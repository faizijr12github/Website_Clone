About Us






The Mindsight Institute, co-founded by Dr. Daniel J. Siegel, and Caroline S. Welch, J.D., is an educational organization offering online learning and in-person workshops that focus on how the development of mindsight in individuals, families and communities can be enhanced by examining the interface of human relationships and basic biological processes


  • We offer a unique description of a healthy mind and how it emerges from INTEGRATION


  • We provide practical steps to strengthen the mind and build integration


  • We offer a definition of the MIND


  • We combine mind, brain, and relationships into one perspective, not found elsewhere


  • We offer a scientifically woven foundation for bringing in all fields of research to understand psychological change


  • We provide a home for ALL forms of therapeutic schools
